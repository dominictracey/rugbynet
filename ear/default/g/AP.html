Note:

  • These player ratings are generated from only match statistics of the 2015-16 Aviva Premiership.
  • As such, injured players or players on international duty see their ratings go down when they don't turn out for their Aviva Premiership side. We have other lists that look across competitions.
  • Recent results are weighted more heavily than early season results. See the link above for details.
  • Individual match ratings are scaled based on the table standings of the two teams at the time the match was played. See the link above for details.
  • Players who play multiple positions are rated independently for each position and may appear on multiple lists.
